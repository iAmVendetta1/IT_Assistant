In the Entra Admin Center
	1. Go to https://entra.microsoft.com
	2. In the left navigation, open Entra ID (This is the “Identity” section — just renamed)
	3. Select Users
	4. Choose the user you want to reset MFA for
	5. Open Authentication methods
	6. Select Require re-register MFA or delete their existing methods

Step 1: Set up the Microsoft Authenticator app (recommended)
They will be guided through:
	• Installing Microsoft Authenticator on their phone
	• Opening the app and choosing Add account → Work or school
	• Scanning the QR code shown on their computer screen
Once scanned, the app will show a 6‑digit code or push notifications.
They click Next to verify.
Step 2: Add a backup method (phone or SMS)
Microsoft will ask them to add a second method, such as:
	• Phone call
	• Text message
	• Alternate email (if allowed by your org)
They follow the prompts and verify the code sent to their phone.
Final Step: MFA setup complete
Once both methods are verified, they click Done and are taken into their account.
