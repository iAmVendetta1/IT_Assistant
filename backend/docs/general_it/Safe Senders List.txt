1. Sign in to Proofpoint at: https://us1.proofpointessentials.com/app/login.php
2. Select the Client from the list or search in the top right. 
3. Expand the Email dropdown in the left-hand navigation pane. 
4. Click Allow List.
5. Click Add Allow Entry in the top right-hand corner.
6. Enter Email address, IP, or domain. 
7. Click Save.
