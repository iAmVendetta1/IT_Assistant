Microsoft Phone Activation Support

This policy applies to all Microsoft Windows and Office products sold by SoftwareDeals that support activation by phone.
Purpose

This policy explains when and how customers may use Microsoft’s phone-based activation process to validate their licensed Microsoft software purchased from SoftwareDeals. Our goal is to ensure every customer has a genuine, fully functional product that successfully activates through Microsoft’s official systems.

Overview

Microsoft provides two activation methods for most Windows and Office products:
1. Online activation through Microsoft’s activation servers, and
2. Phone activation through the Microsoft Activation Center.
Both methods confirm the authenticity of your product key directly with Microsoft. Phone activation remains a supported option within the United States for legitimate retail and volume-based product keys.

When to Use Phone Activation

Customers should use Microsoft’s phone activation system if:
· Online activation fails, system is not connected to internet source, or times out due to network or server issues.
· A major hardware change (for example, motherboard replacement) prevents automatic reactivation.
· The product key was previously activated on another device but is now being moved to a new system (permitted under retail licensing).
· You receive an error stating that the key cannot be validated online.

How to Activate by Phone
1. Open Settings → System → Activation.
2. Select Troubleshoot, then Activate by phone (option appears if online activation fails).
3. Record the Installation ID displayed on screen.
4. Call the official Microsoft Activation Center for the U.S. at 1-888-352-7140.
5. Follow the automated prompts.
6. Enter the Installation ID when prompted.
7. Microsoft will provide a Confirmation ID.
8. Enter it in the activation window to complete the process.
Once complete, Windows will display “Windows is activated with a digital license.”

Important Notes
· Only Microsoft can approve or deny phone activations. SoftwareDeals does not control or influence Microsoft’s activation servers.
· This feature applies only to retail, volume, and transferable licenses.
· OEM (System Builder) keys cannot be activated by phone.
· If you have purchased your product from SoftwareDeals and both online and phone activation fail, SoftwareDeals guarantees to provide a replacement product key that activates successfully, for as long as the manufacturer supports the product.
