Title: Common Xerox Printer Issues and Resolutions

Overview:
This document outlines frequently encountered issues with Xerox multifunction printers and the recommended troubleshooting steps. These steps apply to most Xerox WorkCentre, VersaLink, and AltaLink models.

1. Print Jobs Stuck in Queue
Symptoms:
- Jobs remain in “Processing” or “Pending”
- Printer does not respond to new jobs

Resolution:
- Clear the print queue on the workstation
- Power cycle the printer (wait 30 seconds before turning it back on)
- Verify the printer’s IP address has not changed
- Reinstall the Xerox Global Print Driver if the issue persists

2. Smudged or Streaked Prints
Symptoms:
- Vertical streaks
- Toner smudges
- Uneven print density

Resolution:
- Run the built‑in Cleaning Cycle from the printer menu
- Remove and reseat toner cartridges
- Inspect the imaging unit for toner buildup
- Replace the drum cartridge if streaks persist

3. Scan to Email Fails
Symptoms:
- “SMTP Authentication Failed”
- “Cannot connect to server”

Resolution:
- Verify SMTP server address and port
- Ensure SSL/TLS settings match the mail provider
- Confirm the service account password has not expired
- Test using a different email address to rule out mailbox issues

4. Paper Jams in Tray 1 or Bypass Tray
Symptoms:
- “Paper Jam” message even after clearing paper
- Frequent jams in the same tray

Resolution:
- Remove all paper and fan the stack
- Ensure paper guides are snug but not tight
- Check for torn scraps of paper inside the feed path
- Replace worn feed rollers

5. Color Misalignment
Symptoms:
- Colors appear shifted or blurry
- Misaligned text or graphics

Resolution:
- Run Color Registration Adjustment from the admin menu
- Update printer firmware
- Replace the transfer belt
