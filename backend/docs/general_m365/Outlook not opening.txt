If Outlook isn't opening or it opens but immediately crashes, I have found that disabling the Teams add-in can resolve this. 

1. CTRL + R or search "run" and hit enter. 
2. Type in "Outlook.exe /safe" (without the quotation marks)
3. This opens Outlook with all add-ins disabled. 
4. File > Options > Com Add-ins > Click Go > Uncheck Teams Add-in. 
5. Close and attempt to reopen Outlook. 

It should open this time.
