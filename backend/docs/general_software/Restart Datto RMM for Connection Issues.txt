If we are unable to connect to an online device via Datto RMM, we can have the user perform the following steps to possibly repair the connection. 

1. In the far right section of the taskbar at the bottom of the screen, click the up carat. (The same character as if you were to press SHIFT + 6)
2. Right click the blue circle with a single squiggly white line. 
3. Click restart. 

After a few moments, reattempt connection.
